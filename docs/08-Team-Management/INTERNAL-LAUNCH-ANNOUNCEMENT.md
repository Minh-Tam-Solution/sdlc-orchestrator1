## Introducing SDLC Orchestrator

**The First Governance-First Platform on SDLC 4.9**

---

### What is SDLC Orchestrator?

SDLC Orchestrator is a **bridge-layer tool** that helps teams follow proven SDLC practices by enforcing quality gates, collecting evidence, and ensuring accountability - WITHOUT replacing your existing tools (GitHub, Jira, Linear).

**Core Value Proposition**: Reduce feature waste from **60-70% → <30%** by enforcing evidence-based development practices.

---

### Why This Matters

**The Problem**:
- 60-70% of features built are never used
- Teams skip important validation steps
- Evidence is scattered across tools
- No accountability for quality gates

**The Solution**:
- Structured quality gates (10 stages)
- Centralized evidence vault
- Automated policy enforcement
- Clear approval workflows

---

### Key Features

#### 1. Dashboard
Real-time overview of your projects, gates, and progress.

#### 2. Projects
Create and manage projects with linked GitHub repos.

#### 3. Quality Gates
10 gate types from Problem Definition (G0.1) to Production Readiness (G5).

#### 4. Evidence Vault
Secure storage with SHA256 integrity verification.

#### 5. Policy Engine
110+ pre-built policies powered by Open Policy Agent.
