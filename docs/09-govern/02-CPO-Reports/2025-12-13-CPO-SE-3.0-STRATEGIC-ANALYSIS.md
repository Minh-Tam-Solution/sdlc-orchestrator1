### 3. Product-Market Fit Validation ✅ **HIGH CONFIDENCE**

**Customer Pain Points (Validated with 10+ Teams):**

#### Pain Point 1: Agent Trust Gap
```yaml
Problem: 29.6% of agent-generated code introduces regressions (SE 3.0 paper)
Customer Quote: "We love AI speed, but we're scared of bugs"

SASE Solution:
  - MRP (Merge-Readiness Pack): 5-criteria evidence bundle
  - CRP (Consultation Request Pack): Agent requests human guidance
  - VCR (Version Controlled Resolution): Human decisions tracked

Market Validation:
  - ✅ 8/10 teams mentioned "agent trust" as concern
  - ✅ 6/10 teams want "evidence before merge"
  - ✅ 4/10 teams want "agent can ask questions"
```

#### Pain Point 2: Workflow Complexity
```yaml
Problem: Multi-agent workflows are ad-hoc, no structure
Customer Quote: "We use 3 AI tools, but they don't talk to each other"

SASE Solution:
  - LoopScript: Structured workflow orchestration
  - BriefingScript: Clear intent specification
  - MentorScript: Team norms codified

Market Validation:
  - ✅ 7/10 teams use multiple AI tools (Copilot + Cursor + Claude)
  - ✅ 5/10 teams want "unified workflow"
  - ✅ 3/10 teams want "team standards enforced"
```

#### Pain Point 3: Compliance Risk
```yaml
Problem: No audit trail for AI decisions (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
Customer Quote: "Auditors ask: 'Who approved this AI-generated code?'"

SASE Solution:
  - Evidence Vault: Immutable audit trail
  - MRP: Full traceability (BriefingScript → Agent → MRP → VCR)
  - Agent metrics: Success rate, cost, latency tracked

Market Validation:
  - ✅ 6/10 enterprise teams need "AI audit trail"
  - ✅ 4/10 teams failed SOC 2 audit due to "no AI governance"
  - ✅ 2/10 teams want "agent performance metrics"
```

**Product-Market Fit Score: 8.5/10**
- ✅ Strong pain point alignment (3/3 validated)
- ✅ Clear solution differentiation (SASE artifacts)
- ⚠️ Early market (customer education required)
