## 🌍 Universal Framework Overview

The **SDLC Universal Framework** provides a **scalable AI+Human collaborative development methodology** that adapts to any project size - from solo developers using Claude/ChatGPT to enterprise teams with complex oversight needs.

**Core Innovation**: **FLEXIBLE VERSION SYSTEM** allowing teams to choose the appropriate complexity level while maintaining consistent AI+Human collaboration principles across all scales.

**For Solo Developers**: Simple AI+Human workflows with essential quality gates  
**For Small Teams**: Collaborative processes with peer review and basic oversight  
**For Medium Teams**: Structured processes with role-based development and quality assurance
