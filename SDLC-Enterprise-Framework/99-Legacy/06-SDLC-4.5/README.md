## Historical Context

SDLC 4.6 served the organization well with its Zero Facade Tolerance (ZFT) policy and 4-Layer Oversight system. However, the framework lacked explicit testing standards, leading to the BFlow Platform crisis where 679 mock instances contaminated 26.1% of the test suite.

## Upgrade Rationale

The emergency upgrade to SDLC 4.6 was approved by CPO with a $50K investment to prevent $500K+ catastrophic deployment failures. Key enhancements include:

- **Testing Standards Integration (TSI)**
- **Zero Mock Testing (ZMT) Policy** 
- **Test Quality Gates (TQG)**
- **Enhanced Mock Detection (EMD)**
- **90% Operational Score Requirement**
