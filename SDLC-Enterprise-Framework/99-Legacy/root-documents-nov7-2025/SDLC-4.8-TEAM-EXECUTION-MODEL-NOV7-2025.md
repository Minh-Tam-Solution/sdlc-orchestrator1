```yaml
✅ Daily task prioritization and work allocation
```


#### **Chairman Escalation Points**

```yaml
✅ Quality standard compromises affecting deliverable goals
✅ External partnership or vendor relationship changes
```

```yaml

════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

```yaml
Quality Gate 1: Friday Nov 22 (Design Thinking Foundation)
Quality Gate 2: Friday Dec 6 (Code Review Excellence)  
Quality Gate 3: Friday Dec 20 (Framework Enhancement)
Quality Gate 4: Friday Jan 3 (Integration & Testing)
Quality Gate 5: Friday Dec 27 (Final Launch Authorization)

Format: 
  ✅ Deliverable demonstration and walk-through
  ✅ Quality criteria validation checklist
  ✅ Stakeholder feedback collection and response
  ✅ Go/No-Go decision for next phase
  ✅ Risk assessment and mitigation plan update

Authority: CPO approval required to proceed (Chairman for Gate 5)
```

## 🚨 **ESCALATION PROCEDURES**

### **Immediate Escalation Triggers**
```yaml
Escalate to CPO Immediately:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════
⚠️ Any blocker preventing progress for >24 hours
⚠️ Quality concerns affecting deliverable standards
⚠️ Resource conflicts or availability issues  
⚠️ Scope ambiguity requiring strategic clarification
⚠️ Timeline risks threatening milestone completion
⚠️ Technical issues requiring external expertise
⚠️ Team conflicts affecting productivity

Communication: Slack/email + phone call within 2 hours
Response SLA: CPO response within 4 hours (business days)
```

### **Chairman Escalation Triggers**
```yaml
Escalate to Chairman (via CPO):
════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🔥 Project-stopping blockers (>48 hours delay)
🔥 Budget variance requiring >10% increase
🔥 Timeline extension beyond approved milestones
🔥 Quality compromises threatening framework integrity
🔥 External stakeholder conflicts requiring intervention
🔥 Strategic direction questions beyond CPO authority

Communication: CPO → Chairman escalation memo + meeting request
Response SLA: Chairman response within 24 hours
```

### **CPO (Chief Product Officer) - Strategic Oversight**
```yaml
Strategic Leadership:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════
✅ Design Thinking methodology implementation (Week 1-2 hands-on)
✅ Framework alignment with business objectives validation
✅ Quality standards definition and enforcement
✅ Cross-functional coordination and stakeholder management
✅ Budget oversight and variance approval (within limits)
✅ Timeline management and milestone validation

Quality Gate Authority:
✅ Review and approve all deliverables before progression
✅ Validate Design Thinking integration quality (Gate 1)
✅ Ensure universal framework principle adherence (Gate 2-4) 
✅ Authorize final implementation and launch readiness (Gate 4)
✅ Present final results to Chairman for launch authorization (Gate 5)

Time Investment:
✅ Week 1-2: 25% time (Design Thinking lead implementation)
✅ Week 3-5: 15% time (oversight and validation)
✅ Quality Gates: 100% participation (5 sessions × 2 hours)
✅ Weekly Reviews: 30 minutes + preparation time

Principle: "Strategic guidance without micromanagement"
```

### **Team Accountability Framework**
```yaml
Mutual Accountability:
════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Local Team → CPO:
  "We commit to execute within approved framework,
   meet quality standards, escalate blockers early"

CPO → Local Team:  
  "I commit to provide strategic guidance, remove obstacles,
   approve decisions promptly, enable your success"

Chairman → Project:
  "I trust the team to execute, will review at quality gates,
   and provide final authorization when ready"

Success Metric: All parties enabled to contribute their highest value
Failure Prevention: Clear escalation paths and response commitments
```
