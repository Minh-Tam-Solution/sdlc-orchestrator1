## 🎯 **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

The **MTS SDLC Framework** is designed as a **universal development framework** that can be applied to any project, regardless of industry, technology, or methodology. This guide explains how to use the framework universally while accessing project-specific content.

### 🏆 **FRAMEWORK UNIVERSALITY PRINCIPLES**

1. **Universal Applicability**: Works for any project type and industry
2. **Methodology Agnostic**: Not tied to specific methodologies (MVV, Agile, etc.)
3. **Technology Neutral**: Applicable to any tech stack
4. **Scalable**: Works for teams of 1 to 100+ developers
5. **Adaptable**: Can be customized for specific project needs

---

## 🔍 **HOW TO USE THE FRAMEWORK**

### **📋 Step 1: Choose Appropriate SDLC Version**
Based on your project size and complexity:

| Project Type | Team Size | Complexity | Recommended Version |
|--------------|-----------|------------|---------------------|
| **Learning/Prototype** | 1-2 | Simple | SDLC 3.3.3 |
| **Small Business App** | 2-4 | Basic | SDLC 3.4.0 |
| **Modern Web App** | 4-6 | Moderate | SDLC 3.5 |
| **Enterprise Module** | 6-10 | Complex | SDLC 3.6 |
| **Large Platform** | 10-15 | Advanced | SDLC 3.7.3 |
| **Industry Leader** | 15+ | Revolutionary | SDLC 4.0 |

### **📋 Step 2: Apply Universal Framework Principles**
1. **Design-Before-Code**: Always design before implementation
2. **Documentation-First**: Document as you develop
3. **Quality Gates**: Implement quality controls
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Iterate and improve
5. **Team Collaboration**: Foster team independence

### **📋 Step 3: Customize for Your Project**
- **Methodology**: Choose your preferred methodology (Agile, Waterfall, etc.)
- **Technology**: Apply to your chosen tech stack
- **Industry**: Adapt to your industry requirements
- **Team Culture**: Customize for your team's culture
