## 🎯 What is CodeRabbit?

### Think of it as:
> "A senior AI developer that reviews 100% of your PRs in 2 minutes, catches bugs before human review, and never gets tired or cranky."

### What it does:
- ✅ **Instant PR analysis** - Reviews your PR in 2 minutes vs waiting hours for human
- ✅ **Smart suggestions** - Finds bugs, performance issues, security problems
- ✅ **Interactive chat** - Ask questions, get explanations in PR comments
- ✅ **Learning system** - Gets better over time based on our feedback
- ✅ **VSCode integration** - Quick checks while you code (optional)

### What it does NOT do:
- ❌ Auto-merge PRs (human always decides)
- ❌ Replace human reviewers (just makes them faster)
- ❌ Know our business logic (that's still human domain)
- ❌ Make architectural decisions (we still own that)

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Will AI replace human code reviewers?

**A: No.** CodeRabbit is an assistant, not a replacement. It handles 90% of mechanical checks (syntax, patterns, security) so humans can focus on 10% high-value decisions (architecture, business logic). Think: "AI does the boring stuff, humans do the creative stuff."

---

### Q: What if CodeRabbit makes mistakes?

**A: That's why we pilot!** AI tools aren't perfect. They have ~5-10% false positive rate. You can dismiss wrong suggestions with a reason, and it learns over time. Human reviewers always have final say.

---

### Q: Will this slow down my development?

**A: Opposite goal - make you faster!** Setup might take 30 minutes. First few PRs might feel slower (learning). But goal is 50% faster reviews overall. If it's slowing you down after Week 1, we'll stop.

---

### Q: What about our Zero Mock Policy?

**A: Critical priority!** CTO is personally configuring custom rules to enforce Zero Mock Policy. We'll test extensively. If it misses even ONE mock violation, that's a no-go criterion.

---

### Q: What happens to my code/PRs?

**A: Security is key.** We're using a non-sensitive repository for pilot. CTO is conducting security audit tomorrow (Oct 14). If audit fails, pilot stops immediately. CodeRabbit is SOC 2 certified (industry standard).

---

### Q: Do I need to install anything?

**A: Optional VSCode extension only.** 
- **Required**: Nothing (CodeRabbit reviews PRs automatically in GitHub)
- **Optional**: VSCode extension for quick local checks (recommended)
- **5 minutes** to install, easy to uninstall

---

### Q: What if I hate it?

**A: Tell us!** Your honest feedback is the whole point. If it's frustrating, say so. If majority hate it after 2 weeks, we'll reject it. No hard feelings, we learned something valuable.

---

### Q: What's the long-term plan?

**A: Depends on pilot results.**

**If successful (meets metrics)**:
- Week 3-4: Expand to full team (6 developers)
- Month 2-3: Optimize and tune rules
- Month 4+: Standard tool in SDLC 4.7

**If unsuccessful**:
- Document lessons learned
- Try alternative approach
- No blame, just learning

---

### Q: How much time commitment?

**Daily**:

- Brief check-in with CTO (~5 min)

**Weekly**:
- Feedback session (~30 min)
- Metrics review (~15 min)

**Total**: ~1 hour/week extra (after initial setup)
