### Use Manual Templates When:
- ✅ Learning Design Thinking for first time
- ✅ Teaching methodology to team
- ✅ Complex enterprise projects requiring paper trail
- ✅ Regulatory compliance needs manual documentation
- ✅ Facilitating group workshops (in-person)
- ✅ No AI tools available

### Use AI Acceleration When:
- ✅ Production development (speed matters)
- ✅ Solo or small team (2-5 people)
- ✅ Multiple features per sprint
- ✅ Budget conscious (save 25 hours per feature)
- ✅ Remote teams (async workflows)
- ✅ Already familiar with methodology

### Use Hybrid Approach When:
- ✅ Critical features (quality + speed)
- ✅ Training new team members (learn + produce)
- ✅ Complex domains (need depth + breadth)
- ✅ Stakeholder presentations (manual docs + AI insights)
- ✅ Quality assurance (AI + human validation)
