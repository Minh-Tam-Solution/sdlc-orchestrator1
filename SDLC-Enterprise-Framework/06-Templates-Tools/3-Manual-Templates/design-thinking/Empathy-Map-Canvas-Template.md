# 🧠 Empathy Map Canvas Template

**Purpose**: Deeply understand users' thoughts, feelings, actions, and pain points
**When to Use**: EMPATHIZE phase (SDLC Stage 00 - WHY?)
**Time Required**: 30-45 minutes per user persona
**Participants**: Product team, designers, researchers

---

## 📋 How to Use This Template

1. **Conduct 5-10 user interviews** first (don't guess!)
2. **Fill out this canvas** for each distinct user persona
3. **Synthesize patterns** across multiple users
4. **Share with team** to build shared understanding
5. **Reference during** DEFINE, IDEATE, PROTOTYPE phases

## 🎯 EMPATHY MAP QUADRANTS

### 1. 👁️ WHAT DO THEY SEE?

**Their environment, what they encounter daily, what they observe**

```
Physical Environment:
- [What does their workspace look like?]
- [What tools/systems do they interact with?]
- [What do they see colleagues doing?]

Information Sources:
- [What reports/dashboards do they review?]
- [What communications do they receive?]
- [What problems do they witness?]
```

**Example (NQH-Bot - Lan)**:
```
Physical Environment:
- Staff clocking in on paper forms (messy, prone to errors)
- Stacks of attendance sheets on desk
- Colleagues manually reconciling hours in Excel
- Security camera footage when disputes arise

Information Sources:
- Daily attendance reports (2 hours to compile)
- Payroll errors from accounting team
- Staff complaints about wrong hours recorded
- Manager peers struggling with same issues
```

### 2. 👂 WHAT DO THEY HEAR?

**Influences from friends, colleagues, bosses, market, industry**

```
From Boss/Leadership:
- [What expectations/pressures from management?]
- [What KPIs are they measured on?]
- [What strategic priorities?]

From Peers/Colleagues:
- [What do colleagues complain about?]
- [What solutions are peers trying?]
- [What industry trends are discussed?]

From Customers/Users:
- [What feedback do they receive?]
- [What requests/complaints?]
- [What praise or criticism?]
```

**Example (NQH-Bot - Lan)**:
```
From Boss/Leadership:
- "Control labor costs - we're overspending on payroll"
- "Revenue is down 15% - we need operational efficiency"
- "I need accurate data to make decisions"

From Peers/Colleagues:
- "I don't trust the attendance data anymore"
- "I spend 2 hours daily just tracking who showed up"
- "Some staff are gaming the system"

From Staff:
- "The paper system lost my hours again"
- "Why did I get paid less this month?"
- "The clock-in process is confusing"
```

### 3. 💭 WHAT DO THEY THINK AND FEEL?

**Worries, aspirations, beliefs, attitudes**

#### Thoughts (Rational Mind):
```
About Current Situation:
- [What do they believe is the problem?]
- [What solutions are they considering?]
- [What tradeoffs are they evaluating?]

About Desired Future:
- [What does success look like to them?]
- [What would make their job easier?]
- [What impact do they want to have?]
```

#### Feelings (Emotional State):
```
Frustrations:
- [What makes them angry/frustrated?]
- [What keeps them up at night?]
- [What feels unfair or wrong?]

Hopes/Desires:
- [What do they wish for?]
- [What would bring them joy?]
- [What would make them proud?]

Fears/Anxieties:
- [What are they afraid of?]
- [What risks concern them?]
- [What failure scenarios worry them?]
```

**Example (NQH-Bot - Lan)**:
```
Thoughts (Rational Mind):
About Current Situation:
- "Manual tracking is inefficient but I don't know what else to do"
- "Digital solutions exist but they're probably expensive"
- "I need buy-in from staff - they resist change"

About Desired Future:
- "I want to spend time on strategy, not attendance tracking"
- "Accurate data would help me optimize schedules"
- "If I solve this, I could apply it to other locations"

Feelings (Emotional State):
Frustrations:
- Angry when payroll errors cause staff complaints
- Frustrated spending 10 hours/week on manual work
- Resentful that security footage review is necessary

Hopes/Desires:
- Wishes for a system staff actually use correctly
- Wants to be seen as an effective manager
- Desires work-life balance (not working weekends on reconciliation)

Fears/Anxieties:
- Afraid of implementing tech that fails (loss of credibility)
- Worried about staff pushback on new systems
- Anxious about budget approval for solutions
```

```

- 9:30am-10:15am: Manual reconciliation in Excel
- 10:15am-11:00am: Review security footage for discrepancies
- 5:00pm: Submit payroll data (with anxiety about errors)

Problem-Solving Patterns:
- Uses Excel formulas to cross-check data
- Calls peer managers to compare approaches
- Watches YouTube tutorials on "attendance tracking software"
- Delays implementing solutions due to fear of failure
```


**Example (NQH-Bot - Lan)**:
```

   Evidence: "I don't trust the data anymore" (7/10 managers said this)
   Impact: Cannot make strategic decisions, ₫15B revenue at risk

2. Time Waste (2 hours daily)
   Evidence: "Manual tracking takes 2 hours daily" (10/10 managers)
   Impact: 60 hours/month wasted, prevents strategic work

3. Staff Disputes & Morale
   Evidence: "Staff complain about wrong hours" (8/10 managers)
   Impact: Trust erosion, retention risk, manager credibility damaged
```

## ✅ VALIDATION CHECKLIST

Before moving to DEFINE phase:

- [ ] **Evidence-based**: Every statement backed by interview quotes or observations (not assumptions)
- [ ] **Emotional depth**: Captured both functional needs AND emotional needs
- [ ] **Pattern recognition**: Identified patterns across 5+ users (not just one person's opinion)
- [ ] **Team alignment**: Entire team can explain user's perspective in user's own words
- [ ] **Surprising insights**: Found at least 1-2 things that contradicted our initial assumptions

**Red Flags** (Redo EMPATHIZE if true):
- ❌ Based on "we think users need..." instead of "users said they need..."
- ❌ No direct quotes from users
- ❌ Only 1-2 interviews conducted (insufficient sample size)
- ❌ Team members have different interpretations of user needs

## 🤖 AI ASSISTANCE TIPS

**Use Claude/ChatGPT to**:
- Synthesize patterns across multiple interview transcripts
- Identify contradictions in user statements
- Generate follow-up interview questions
- Suggest emotional needs implied by functional needs

**Prompt Example**:
```
I conducted 10 user interviews about [topic]. Here are the transcripts:

[Paste interview notes]

Please help me:
1. Identify top 3 pain points mentioned by 70%+ of users
2. Extract 5 most impactful direct quotes
3. Suggest emotional needs underlying the functional needs expressed
4. Flag any contradictions in what users say vs what they do
```

**Warning**: AI cannot replace real user empathy. Use AI to analyze, not to guess.
