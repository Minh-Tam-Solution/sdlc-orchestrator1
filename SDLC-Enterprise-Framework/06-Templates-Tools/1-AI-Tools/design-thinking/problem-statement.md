## 🚀 Quick Start Prompt

```
Generate problem statement from user insights:

Insights from Empathize Phase:
1. [Insight 1 with evidence]
2. [Insight 2 with evidence]  
3. [Insight 3 with evidence]

User Persona: [describe primary user]
Pain Point: [specific pain identified]
Context: [business/cultural/environmental factors]

Generate:
✅ Problem Statement ("User needs X because Y")
✅ "How Might We" (HMW) Questions (5-7)
✅ Design Challenge Statement
✅ Success Criteria
✅ Constraints to Consider

Format: [User] needs [need] because [insight]
Validate: User-centered, specific, actionable, leaves room for solutions
```

---

## 📋 Problem Statement Template

```
[USER SEGMENT] needs [SPECIFIC NEED/SOLUTION]
because [UNDERLYING INSIGHT/REASON].

Example (Vietnamese SME):
"Vietnamese SME owners need a business software system that staff can learn in 1 week 
because their employees (45-60 years old) have limited computer skills and the business 
cannot afford 3 months of training time."

Validation Checklist:
✅ User-centered (starts with user, not technology)
✅ Specific (clear who, what, why)
✅ Insight-based (grounded in research)
✅ Leaves room for solutions (doesn't prescribe how)
✅ Actionable (team can act on it)
```
